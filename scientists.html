Key Scientists & Leaders

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam was a leading scientist in India's space and missile programs and served as the 11th President of India.

Dr. K. Sivan

ISRO Chairman (2018–2022), oversaw Chandrayaan-2 and several major launches.

Dr. Mylswamy Annadurai

Project Director for Chandrayaan-1 and Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission).

Dr. K. Radhakrishnan

ISRO Chairman (2009–2014), led the Mars Orbiter Mission and several satellite launches.
