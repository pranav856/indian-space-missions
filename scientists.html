Key Scientists & Leaders

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam was a leading scientist in India's space program serving as the director of India's first satellite launch vehicle.

Dr. K. Sivan

ISRO Chairman (2018–2022), oversaw Chandrayaan-2 and several major launches.

Dr. Mylswamy Annadurai

Project Director for Chandrayaan-1 and Mangalyaan (Mars Orbiter Mission).

Dr. K. Radhakrishnan

ISRO Chairman (2009–2014), led the Mars Orbiter Mission and several satellite launches.

Prof. Satish Dhawan

Chairman of ISRO (1972–1984), Dhawan was instrumental in building India's space infrastructure and mentoring future leaders like Dr. Kalam.

Dr. Vikram Sarabhai

Regarded as the father of the Indian space program, Sarabhai laid the foundation for ISRO and championed space science for societal development.

Dr. S. Somanath

led the successful Chandrayaan-3 mission and continues to advance India's space exploration goals.
