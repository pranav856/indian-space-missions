Gaganyaan

  • Planned Launch: 2025
  • Launch Vehicle: Human-rated LVM3
  • Objective: India's first crewed spaceflight
  • Status: Under development

Gaganyaan is India's first human spaceflight mission, aiming to send Indian astronauts (Gagannauts) to low Earth orbit. The project showcases India's capabilities in human space flight and is a major milestone for ISRO.

Key Features

  • Indigenous crew module and service module.
  • Rigorous astronaut selection and training.
  • Advanced safety and abort systems.
