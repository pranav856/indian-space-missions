Astrosat

  • Launch Date: 28 September 2015
  • Launch Vehicle: PSLV-C30
  • Objective: India's first dedicated multi-wavelength space observatory
  • Status: Operational

Astrosat is India's first dedicated multi-wavelength space observatory. It enables simultaneous observations of various astronomical objects in different wavelengths, contributing significantly to high-energy astronomy research.

Key Achievements

  • First Indian space observatory for multi-wavelength astronomy.
  • International collaborations and discoveries.
