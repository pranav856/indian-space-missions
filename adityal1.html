Aditya-L1

  • Launch Date: 2 September 2023
  • Launch Vehicle: PSLV-C57
  • Objective: Study the Sun from L1 point
  • Status: In progress

Aditya-L1 is India’s first solar mission, launched to study the Sun’s corona, solar emissions, and space weather from the Sun-Earth L1 Lagrange point. The mission aims to enhance understanding of solar phenomena and their impact on Earth.

Key Achievements

  • First Indian mission dedicated to solar observation.
  • Operating from L1 point for continuous Sun monitoring.
